Location and accomodation
The reception will be at The Old Lodge, Minchinhampton. There is plenty of parking there, and if need be you can leave a car there over night and collect it in the morning. We're planning on having the ceremony a short walk from there - details to follow when we have them...
Sadly the Old Lodge itself doesn't have much accommodation so even we don't have a room there!
Nearby is the Egypt Mill where some family have already booked.
Stroud has a Premier Inn and Stonehouse a Travelodge. There's lots of small B+Bs in the area too.
If you're on a budget there are a few spare rooms/floors with friends and family about - just ask!
